At the heart of the Whispering Woods, Elara finally uncovered the map’s secret: a hidden spring of ancient magic that had been sealed away to protect both forest and village. With Shadow by her side, she carefully restored its flow, breaking the old curse that had kept the land barren and fearful. In the days that followed, crops flourished, the woods softened into a place of gentle wonder, and the villagers spoke of Elara not as a curious girl who vanished into the trees, but as the one who brought life back to their world.
