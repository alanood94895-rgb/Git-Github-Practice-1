Shadowy sorcerer who feeds on forgotten memories, erasing history to grow stronger, leaving only hollow echoes and broken identities behind.

Elara discovers a scorched parchment fragment in the forest, bearing half of an ancient rune still faintly glowing ominously etched.

Squeaky, a time-traveling squirrel, appears to Elara offering cryptic guidance, tiny gadgets, and chaotic warnings about future branches collapsing unstable.
